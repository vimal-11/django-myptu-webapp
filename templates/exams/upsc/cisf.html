CISF AC (EXE) LDCE 2022 Eligibility Criteria

The candidates must satisfy the eligibility criteria set by the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) in order to apply for the CISF AC (EXE) LDCE Recruitment 2022. The eligibility criteria include nationality, educational qualifications, age criteria and Physical standards.

  • The candidate should be an Indian citizen to apply for the CISF Recruitment 2021.
  • The candidate must have a graduate degree in any stream from a recognized university.

The CISF AC (EXE) LDCE eligibility criteria are discussed below in detail:

CISF AC (EXE) LDCE Eligibility: Age Criteria

The candidate must not have attained the age of 35 years as of August 01, 2021, i.e, the candidate should not have been born before August 02, 1986. However, if a candidate belongs to a Scheduled Caste or a Scheduled Tribe, the upper age limit prescribed above shall be relaxable up to a maximum of five years.

CISF AC (EXE) LDCE Eligibility: Service Requirements

The candidates must have completed 4 years of regular service in the rank of Inspector (GD) or Sub-Inspector (GD) including the basic training period in order to apply for this position. The candidates also must have a clean service report till the issue of the offer of appointment.

CISF AC (EXE) LDCE Eligibility: Number of Attempts

Every eligible candidate gets a maximum of three attempts at the CISF AC (EXE) LDCE 2021 exam.

CISF AC (EXE) LDCE Eligibility: Physical Standards

In order to apply for the CISF AC (EXE) LDCE 2021 exam, the candidate must meet the prescribed Physical and Medical Standards.

CISF AC (EXE) LDCE Syllabus 2022

Part Section Syllabus
Part-A: General Ability and Intelligence General Mental Ability The questions will be designed to test logical reasoning, quantitative aptitude including numerical ability and data interpretation.
General Science The questions will be set to test general awareness, scientific temper, comprehension and appreciation of scientific phenomena of everyday observation including new areas of importance like Information Technology, Biotechnology and Environmental Science.
Current Events of National and International Importance The questions will test the candidates’ awareness of current events of national and international importance in the broad areas of culture, music, arts, literature, sports, governance, societal and developmental issues, industry, business, globalisation and the interplay among nations.
General Awareness The questions shall aim to test candidates’ general awareness and would inter-alia include questions to test basic knowledge of the history of India, Indian and World Geography as well as Indian Polity and Economy.

2.Medical Standard Test

The candidates should meet some of the prescribed medical standards to be eligible for the post of Assistant Commandant. The medical standard test will include the examination of the following body parts or organs:
  • Eyesight
  • Carrying Angle
  • Ear
  • Nose
  • General medical and Speech Standards

CISF AC (EXE) LDCE 2022 Selection: Phase-III

The candidates who will clear the physical and medical tests will be called for an Interview or Personality Test. The selected candidates will be then called for the document verification process.
